Fueling America

Energy Sources of United States
1990 - 2010

In this project, we would like to use d3 javascript library to display the energy sources for the United States from years 1990 to 2010. The energy sources are the following: Wind, Coal, Geothermal, Hydroelectric Conventional, Natural Gas, Other Biomass, Nuclear, Other Gases, Petroleum, Pumped Storage, Solar Thermal and Photovoltaic, Wood and Wood Derived Fuels, and Other.

The original map displays for each state the type of energy source that is used the most. Clicking on a state will update the values on the pie chart to display the percentages of each energy source used in that state. The lower pie chart displays the national percentage of each energy source. Clicking on a legend will display the choropleth map of that specific energy source for each state.
